# GeneticAlgorithmModification
Modification to Genetic Algorithm to mimic Human Thought Process

The purpose of the research is to simulate the Human Thinking Process with a simple approach. My research proposes, the Genetic Algorithm can be used for the same. Basic idea is, 
1. Genetic Algorithm is an optimization algorithm, where from initial random population, fittest individuals are selected which are modified with 'Genetic Operators' to evolve the next Population. The cycle repeates itself till the optimal solution is found.
2. This is analogous to how we think. We, in our conscious start with random thought/s (generated with our five senses or some memories). Out of that, we select the most preferred thought (based on what is important or tempting to us at that point), with the help of which our next thought/s are generated, similar to using 'Genetic Operators' to create new population based on fittest individual. 

In addition, this research proposes a modification to the conventional Genetic Algorithm that enhances its performance in simulating this cognitive process.

To validate the proposed theory, a modified Genetic Algorithm is implemented on the classic **Travelling Salesperson Problem (TSP)**. Since TSP is a well-known NP-hard optimization problem, demonstrating the effectiveness of the proposed approach on TSP suggests that the methodology can be generalized and applied to a wide range of other optimization problems.
